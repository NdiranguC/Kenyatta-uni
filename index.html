KENYATTA UNIVERSITY

Kenyatta University (KU) is a public research university with it's main campus in Nairobi. It acquired the status of university in 1985, being the third university after University of Nairobi (1970) and Moi University (1984). As of 2014, it was one of 23 public universities in the country.
